O, write my name, O, write my name; O, write my name,
The Angels in heav’n going-to write my name.

Write my name when-a you get home, —
The Angels of heav’n going-to write my name.

Yes, write my name with-a golden pen, —
The Angels of heav’n going-to write my name.

Yes, write my name in the Book of Life, —
The Angels of heav’n going-to write my name.

Yes, write my name in the drip-pin’ blood, —
The Angels of heav’n going-to write my name.

O, write my name, O, write my name, O, write my name,
The Angels of heav’n going-to write my name.

Spiritual, Underground Railroad
